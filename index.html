My Story

My name is Adam Laxalt. I come from Reno city and was born on 31 August 1978. I am running for Congress in our beloved state of Nevada as a Republican Party candidate. I am thrilled to be here today and have the opportunity to introduce myself to you and share my vision of this great Silver State. After 9/11 I felt more than ever that my duty was to fight for my country. I have been defending my country from overseas as a Navy and enforcing the law as an Attorney General.
