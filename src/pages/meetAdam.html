My Story

My name is Adam Laxalt. I come from Reno city and was born on 31 August 1978. I am running for Congress in our beloved state of Nevada as a Republican Party candidate. I am thrilled to be here today and have the opportunity to introduce myself to you and share my vision of this great Silver State. After 9/11 I felt more than ever that my duty was to fight for my country. I have been defending my country from overseas as a Navy and enforcing the law as an Attorney General. I have received numerous medals for fighting in Iraq. Although, no medal can compare to the pride of being able to defend my country. No medal could describe the pain of having our fellow Americans lost to enemies. There always has been good and evil in this world and I have always chosen to fight for the good. I have served on the side of my people as an attorney as well - protecting the state’s most vulnerable citizens, keeping its communities safe, and pursuing justice for victims of sexual assault. I’ve committed my life to fight for what’s right because that’s what Americans do. Now the time has come for me to serve my country as a Senator of Nevada. Now the time has come to pick sides! I am a firm believer that God has given everyone a right to life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness. I support the right of citizens to defend their families and kids if the necessity will be. Nobody can take the right of defending our sons and daughters, mother and fathers, wives and husbands. Nobody can take this from us - Americans. Our ancestors and fellow veterans have been doing this and we will do this too. This is essential to the maintenance of a free society and a right I will defend vigorously as your Senator. I believe building a stronger and more prosperous Nevada depends first and foremost on the safety of our communities. As a Senator, I will continue to work tirelessly to protect our state, I will always promote policies that protect the citizens of Nevada from dangerous criminals. I will strongly support our local law enforcement against efforts to make Nevada a sanctuary state. Some people have been trying to do this and I will never let this happen! I am committed to strengthening our economy and believe that Nevada can take greater steps than it has ever taken. People will agree that we are entering a new era of tremendous prosperity. The opportunity is in front of us if we take a forward-thinking approach to developing our economy. My predecessors from other parties have been using their power in the favor of themselves and their supporters. I guarantee that I can bring economic change to Nevada and remove barriers to job creation for everyone. Young people are the future of our nation. If we don’t support them there is no development or progress. Education is the key to our success! For the sake of young Nevadans, it will be my top priority to ensure that no child gets left behind the school doors. We owe this to our children and grandchildren! My Plan for a better Nevada outlines a vision of opportunity, social justice, and prosperity for all. My goal to modernize and expand public infrastructure will be beneficial so people can go to work with better buses and on better roads. I will remove the quotas on the environmental resources as well. Nature gives us the power to make gas prices cheaper and leftists are just saying no to it. Capturing the development of a flourishing economy will influence every ordinary life - we will create more jobs for our people, and make food prices cheaper and available for everyone. I promise to never say no to the wellness of our people! If you think that I am your candidate, support me and the Republican Party and vote for our candidates on the day of the elections. Together we can continue to make America great! Thank you for your time, thank you, everyone! God bless you and God bless America!
